Voice Separator

Voice Separator is an AI-powered vocal separator app that helps you separate vocals from music directly on your iPhone. You can extract vocals, create instrumental tracks, and prepare karaoke-style audio files with a simple and clean interface.

About the App

Voice Separator is designed for singers, musicians, vocal coaches, DJs, karaoke lovers, music students, and content creators who want a fast and easy way to isolate vocals or remove vocals from songs.

Main Features

  • Separate vocals from music using AI
  • Extract vocal tracks from audio files
  • Create instrumental or karaoke-style tracks
  • Preview original and separated audio
  • Save high-quality WAV audio files
  • Simple, clean, and easy-to-use interface
